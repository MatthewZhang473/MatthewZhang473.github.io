About

I am a machine learning researcher at the Computational and Biological Learning Lab (CBL), University of Cambridge. My research interests include Bayesian machine learning, Gaussian processes, graph machine learning, and Monte-Carlo methods.

My work focuses on developing scalable Bayesian inference methods and applying them to real-world graphs. I recently graduated with a BA + MEng degree (First Class Honours) from Cambridge University and have experience working at Myrtle.AI, Microsoft, and Huawei.
