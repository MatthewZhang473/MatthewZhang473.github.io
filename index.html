About

Hey there, welcome to my homepage. I am a machine learning researcher @CBL Cambridge. My research interests include Bayesian machine learning, Gaussian processes, graph machine learning, and Monte-Carlo methods. This page serves as a hub for my academic work, publications, and professional updates.

My research focuses on developing scalable bayesian inference methods and applying them to real-world graphs with over 1M nodes. I am passionate about interdisciplinary collaboration and am always interested in new research opportunities.
