About

I am a machine learning researcher at the Computational and Biological Learning Lab (CBL), University of Cambridge. My research interests include Bayesian machine learning, Gaussian processes, graph machine learning, and Monte-Carlo methods. (See my CV).

My research focuses on developing scalable bayesian inference methods and applying them to real-world graphs with over 1M nodes. I am passionate about interdisciplinary collaboration and am always interested in new research opportunities.
